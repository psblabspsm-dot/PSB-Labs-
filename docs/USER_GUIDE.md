# SURYA CREDIT SOLUTIONS: END-USER PLATFORM GUIDE

Welcome to the **Surya Credit Solutions** user guide. This document provides step-by-step instructions for on-field retailers, agents, and distributors to operate the mobile application and utilize core FinTech and marketplace features safely.

---

### 1.1 First-Time Login Sequence
1. Download and open the **Surya Credit Solutions** application.
2. Enter your registered mobile number and tap **Send OTP**.
3. Enter the 6-digit verification code received on your mobile.
4. Set a strong password and a secure 4-digit **MPIN**. (The MPIN is required to authorize all wallet transfers and payments).

## 2. DOMESTIC MONEY TRANSFER (DMT) WORKFLOW

DMT allows agents to send money instantly to any bank account in India on behalf of customers.

### 2.1 Initiating a DMT Transaction
1. Go to the **Domestic Money Transfer** section on your mobile dashboard.
2. Select **Add Beneficiary** and enter the beneficiary's details:
   - Full Name
   - Bank Name
   - Account Number
   - IFSC Code
3. Select the beneficiary from your list and input the transfer amount.
4. Tap **Submit Transfer**.
5. Review the processing fee and estimated cash receipt.
6. Enter your secure **4-digit MPIN** to authorize the withdrawal.
7. Once the screen shows **Success**, click **Print Receipt** or **Share via WhatsApp** to provide the proof of payment to the customer.

---

## 3. AADHAAR ENABLED PAYMENT SYSTEM (AEPS) CASH WITHDRAWALS

AEPS allows walk-in bank customers to withdraw cash or check balances using biometric fingerprint authentication.

### 3.1 Cash Withdrawal Steps
1. Connect your certified biometric scanner (e.g. Mantra, Morpho) to your mobile device via OTG.
2. Select **AEPS Cash Withdrawal** on the dashboard.
3. Enter the customer's **Aadhaar Number** and select their **Bank Name**.
4. Enter the withdrawal amount (multiples of 100, max ₹10,000 per transaction).
5. Instruct the customer to place their finger on the scanner and click **Scan Biometric**.
6. On a successful fingerprint scan, the cash is instantly debited from the customer's bank account and credited directly to your **Surya Agent Wallet**.
7. Hand over the physical cash to the customer and print their transaction receipt.

---

## 4. RETAIL MARKETPLACE PROCUREMENT

Agents can buy stock and wholesale items directly from regional distributors and vendors using their credit or cash wallets.

### 4.1 Purchasing Products
1. Tap the **Wholesale Marketplace** icon on your screen.
2. Browse the wholesale catalog or filter items by category (e.g. FMCG, Electronics).
3. Add the items you need to your cart and tap **Proceed to Checkout**.
4. Select your preferred payment method:
   - **Trade Wallet Balance**: Pays instantly from your standard wallet.
   - **Credit Line**: Utilizes your pre-approved distributor credit pool.
5. Confirm the shipping address and enter your **MPIN**.
6. Track your shipment status directly in the **Order Tracker** section.

---

## 5. GENERAL SECURITY GUIDELINES FOR USERS

- **Never Share Your MPIN**: Surya Credit Solutions support staff or distributors will never ask for your password or MPIN.
- **Verify Bank Account Numbers**: Double-check bank account numbers and IFSC codes before confirming DMT transfers, as instant transfers cannot be reversed.
- **Sync Local Operations**: If you work in remote areas with unstable network connectivity, ensure you sync your pending local records when returning to high-signal zones.
